Meet the Heroes and Villains

Goku

Son Goku is a Saiyan from planet Vegeta who was raised on Earth, he is constantly looking for ways to get stronger and he's always down for a good fight.

Vegeta

Vegeta, the Prince of all Saiyans, constantly competing with Goku, Vegeta has the highest pride for himself and pushses past his limits, attempting to face each challenge head on like a true warrior.

Frieza

Frieza, the tyrant who destroyed Planet Vegeta, Frieza is one of the first and main villains in Dragon Ball Z, his hate for Saiyains drives him to try and eliminate the race as a whole.
